Data analysis of resumes

Project goals were to create apply NLP and AI tools, models to analyze and compare two sets of resumes to find not obvious patterns, similarities and differences between 2 major group (High VS Low Value Prospect Candidates)

Project Details

I have conducted EDA analysis as a first step to identify relationships and patterns in this 2 groups. Fortunatelly enough, client has provided labeled set of data for around 30 CVs which was extremelly helpfull in EDA and modeling steps. Those insights, allowed powerful feature engineering, fine-tuning of parameters and development of highly accurare model.
